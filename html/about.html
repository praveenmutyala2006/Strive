Our Mission

Strive is more than just a habit tracker. It is a minimalist, dopamine-driven platform designed to hack your psychology for the better.

The Philosophy

We often try to learn a habit to make our personal life better or a skill to make our professional life better. But we tend to fail in that process because of lack of proper tracking and lack of motivation to learn that habit or skill. So this website helps us track our progress in learning what we want and provide us with motivation when needed. Since most people work either on the basis of motivation or temporary moods, they do not have a consistent performance in any skill. Everyone has different ways of being motivated, be it movies, songs or books, or quotations or a creative image. It’s designed to give you that specific push for each task so you can keep moving forward without feeling burnt out.
