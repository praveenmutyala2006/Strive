Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your doubts

What is Strive? +
Strive is a minimalist, dopamine-driven productivity and habit tracking app designed to help you build consistency and discipline through visual momentum.
How does the Momentum Score work? +
Your momentum score increases as you log habits and decreases when you miss them. It's a real-time indicator of your consistency.
Can I customize my habits? +
Yes, you can add any custom habit from your Dashboard. Simply type the habit name and click "Log Habit".
What is the Motivation Engine? +
The Motivation Engine provides curated auditory anchors (like lo-fi or ambient noise) and tools like a deep focus sprint timer and a 60-second breathwork protocol to get you into a flow state.
How does mood-based motivation work? +
You log your mood, and Strive responds with tailored motivation like quotes, reminders, or suggestions to help you take action.
What makes Strive different from other habit trackers?+
Unlike traditional habit trackers that only focus on streaks and statistics, Strive also focuses on motivation and mood-based support. It provides a clean, distraction-free experience while helping users stay consistent through personalized encouragement like quotes, music, books, and creative content. The platform is designed to reduce burnout and make self-improvement feel more natural and sustainable.
